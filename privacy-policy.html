Section 01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Word-by-Word Quran Morphology Portal — an independent academic project published at morphology.kanzulilm.com.

The Portal is developed, maintained, and directed by Mufti Syed Muhammad Usman Bukhari, founding director of Kanz ul Ilm International — an Islamic educational research institution established in 2009. Our work is motivated by a sincere commitment to making the Quranic sciences accessible to students, researchers, and seekers of knowledge across the world, regardless of their background or resources.

This Portal presents the complete word-by-word morphological and grammatical analysis of the Holy Qur'an — all 114 Surahs, 6,236 verses, and over 80,000 words — entirely free of charge. Operational costs are partially met through minimal advertising revenue, as disclosed fully in this policy.

For the purposes of data protection law, Kanz ul Ilm International is the data controller for this Portal.

Section 03

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

A cookie is a small file that a website places on your device to remember information about your visit. This Portal itself sets no first-party cookies of its own. However, the third-party services we use — particularly Google AdSense — do set cookies.

Cookies Set by Third Parties

  • Google Advertising Cookies — used to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history across the web. These are the most significant cookies on this Portal.
  • Google Fonts Cookies — a minimal technical cookie that may be set when the Arabic and English fonts are loaded from Google's servers.
  • Cloudflare Cookies — used for security, performance, and bot detection (e.g. the __cf_bm cookie).

Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit this Portal, a consent banner appears at the bottom of your screen. You may choose to Accept or Decline non-essential cookies. You can also manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are set
  • Block all or certain cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Please note that declining advertising cookies will not remove ads from the Portal — it will only make them less relevant to your interests. For industry-wide opt-out tools, visit aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu.

Section 07

Data Retention

Because this Portal does not operate its own database or server-side data storage, we do not retain any personal data ourselves.

Data retained by third-party services is governed by their own retention policies. As a general reference:

  • Google: Retains advertising interaction data for up to 18 months by default, though this is configurable in your Google account settings.
  • Cloudflare: Retains server logs for a limited period (typically up to 24 hours for free plans) for security and performance analysis.

Cookies stored on your own device persist for varying durations depending on the cookie type — from session cookies that expire when you close your browser, to persistent cookies that may last up to 2 years (in the case of some advertising cookies). You may delete these at any time through your browser settings.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

This Portal is a scholarly Quranic reference tool suitable for all ages, including young students of Islamic studies. We welcome learners of every background and age.

However, we do not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from children under the age of 13, or under the applicable age of digital consent in their jurisdiction (16 in many European countries under GDPR).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information through contact email, please write to us at info@kanzulilm.com and we will take immediate steps to address your concern.

Section 11

European Users — GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or applicable national equivalents apply to the processing of your personal data.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Legitimate Interest — We have a legitimate interest in serving advertising to fund this free academic resource, balanced against your right to privacy. Where this applies, we rely on the consent mechanism (cookie banner) to give you meaningful control.
  • Consent — For non-essential cookies and personalized advertising, we rely on your consent as expressed through the cookie consent banner displayed when you first visit the Portal.
  • Legal Obligation — Where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law.

Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — obtain a copy of personal data held about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how your data is used
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw Consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@kanzulilm.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

Section 12

California Residents — CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information.

Your CCPA Rights

  • Right to Know — what personal information is collected, used, or shared
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of personal information we hold
  • Right to Opt-Out — opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights
Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: Kanz ul Ilm International does not sell personal information. We do share limited technical data with Google for advertising purposes, which may constitute "sharing" under CCPA. You may opt out of this through Google Ads Settings or the cookie consent banner on this Portal.

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at info@kanzulilm.com with the subject line "CCPA Request." We will respond within 45 days.

Section 13

Security

We take the security of this Portal seriously and have implemented appropriate technical measures, including:

  • HTTPS encryption — all data exchanged between your browser and this Portal is encrypted in transit using TLS.
  • Cloudflare security layer — protection against DDoS attacks, malicious bots, and other web threats.
  • No server-side database — since the Portal holds no user data of its own, there is no database to breach.

While we have taken reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure. We encourage you to use an up-to-date browser and to exercise your own good judgment when sharing any information online.
